Welcome to New Product Registration

Please follow the instructions below to complete your product registration. Each step requires specific details, so make sure to have the required information ready.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  • Step 1: Product Information

    Provide details about the product, including its name, version, launch date, status, and description. Ensure all required fields are filled in accurately.

  • Step 2: Business Value

    Rate the business impact of the product across several metrics such as decision-making, monetization, and collaboration. Use the sliders to input your ratings.

  • Step 3: Product Owners

    Add product owner details, including their name, ZID, and email. Multiple product owners can be added as necessary.

  • Step 4: Snowflake Data Sources

    Specify details about the Snowflake data sources, including database, schema, and table names. Ensure the data types are accurately identified.

  • Step 5: Other Data Sources

    Provide details for other data sources used in the product, including dataset descriptions and types.

  • Step 6: Platform Information

    Include platform details such as platform name and description. Ensure all information is accurate.

Important Notes:

All fields marked as required must be filled in to proceed. Use the "Save" button on each page to save your progress. You can navigate back to any step if changes are needed before submission.
